TANZANIA

Kiwalani community in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, is an urban informal settlement. Water quantity is sufficient but most of the water sources are severely contaminated. We visited the community three times during the past three years and distributed 15 filters in collaboration with SON international, which is BioSand Filter (BSF) producer in Tanzania. AQUA-BRIDGE and SON international will continue the collaboration.

COLLABORATOR: SON international (son-international.org) is a non-profit organization started in 2003 by American Christian missionary to serve people of Tanzania. Their focus is to establish a biosand filter workshop and training center so that local people can be trained to learn the importance of clean water and how to obtain clean water using biosand filtration. SON international and AQUA-BRIDGE collaborated during past two years and successfully distribution 15 biosand filters to the community. We are also in collaboration to improve the filtration system.
