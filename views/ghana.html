Ghana

Tamale community in Ghana is a rural region with dispersed setting. People are suffering from highly turbid and microbiologically contaminated water. Pure Home Water (PHW) has been producing and distributing Ceramic Pot Filters (CPF). AQUA-BRIDGE and PHW agreed to collaborate to better serve the community.

Hilton Foundation. In 2012, PHW is engaged in expanding our state-of-the-art ceramic water filter factory in Tamale, Ghana to produce AfriClay Filters, our principal product (formerly known as the Kosim Filter). Susan Murcott has been the mentor of AQUA-BRIDGE for past three years and we also have ongoing research collaboration to improve ceramic pot filter performance.
