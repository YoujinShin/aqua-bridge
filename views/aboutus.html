WHAT WE DO

1. Support local filter manufacturers and distributors in developing countries to monitor their filter performances.

2. Develop effective data collection, management and analysis system that leads to data driven decision for tackling drinking water crisis.

3. Provide easy and accessible method to test one’s own water source and share with general public as a world water quality map.


HOW WE DO

We have expertise and extensive experience in low-cost water filtration technology, water quality testing and data management system.


FILTRATION TECHNOLOGY

BioSand Filter (BSF) and Ceramic Pot Filter (CPF) are the two main filtration technologies which AQUA-BRIDGE’s collaborators produce and distribute. BSF and CPF are known to be one of the most effective methods for delivering clean water.

WATER QUALITY TESTING

AQUA-BRIDGE uses the EC-Kit which is a simple, inexpensive field water quality test kit. EC-Kit is developed by Susan Murcott lab at MIT and consists of Colilert™ and 3M Petrifilm™ tests.

SMART DATA MANAGEMENT

Our innovative web-based ICT platform enables to effectively integrate, manage and analyze the gathered data to quantify impact and make data-driven decisions.
