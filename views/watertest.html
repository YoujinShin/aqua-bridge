Let's learn how to test water quality


Petrifilm test

First, drop 1ml of water on the petrifilm

Second, wait for 24 hours

Third, count the number of blue & red dots

(blue: bad bacteria, red: other bacteria)




Colilert test

First, drop 10ml of your water sample into the test tube

Second, wait for 24 hours

Third, check the color of water

(fluorescent: bad bacteria, yellow: other bacteria)
